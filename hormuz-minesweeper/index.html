Strait of Hormuz Minesweeper

Clear the mines from the world's most contested waterway

Roughly 20% of the world's oil passes through the Strait of Hormuz, a narrow chokepoint between Iran and Oman. It has been mined in wartime, threatened in peacetime, and remains one of the most strategically important passages on Earth.

Your job: sweep the strait clear. Left-click to reveal water. Right-click to flag a mine. The numbers tell you how many mines are adjacent. Don't hit one.
